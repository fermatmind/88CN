# 09 Editorial Pipeline

The editorial pipeline separates AI drafting from human approval.

## AI Drafting

AI may create drafts for internal review. Drafts are not public content.

AI output must stay grounded in public sources, founder submissions, and existing verified data.

## Human Review

A public Editorial Note requires human review and approval. Reviewers must confirm source quality, wording, and unknown-data handling.

## Data Boundary

Editorial content must not invent metrics, customers, financing claims, rankings, partnerships, or private facts. Unknown information must stay visibly unknown.
