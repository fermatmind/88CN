# 00 Project Brief

88CN is the Free AI Project Growth Index.

## Phase 1 Definition

Phase 1 creates a public growth-signal index for free AI projects. It supports project discovery, founder claims, editorial review, and future structured growth products.

## Product Boundaries

Phase 1 focuses on:

- public project profiles
- public growth signals
- claim and review workflows
- editor-selected discovery
- explainable scoring
- staged public data import

Phase 1 does not support automatic publication, private-data ingestion, paid ranking influence, or unreviewed claims.

## Operating Principles

- Public pages must separate verified facts from editorial judgment.
- Unknown facts must be shown as unknown.
- Human review is required before public editorial notes and lifecycle promotion.
- Public data sources must stay traceable.
- Paid exposure must never affect Signal Score.
