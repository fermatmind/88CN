## Launch Gate

Every future implementation task must prove:

1. route family is explicitly allowlisted;
2. dynamic params are finite and reviewed;
3. data source is reviewed/published only;
4. lifecycle states are allowed;
5. copy passes public-language boundary;
6. indexability and sitemap state match threshold;
7. canonical ownership is defined;
8. Featured Signals or sponsorship cannot alter organic systems;
9. under-threshold routes fall back to `noindex, follow`;
10. no product route is created before its task.
