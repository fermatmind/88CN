# TRAFFIC2 Landscape Copy and CTA Policy

Result: GO_TRAFFIC2B

## Scope

This file defines future copy and CTA policy for `/landscape`. It does not create public page copy, route code, components, or assets.

## Approved Positioning

Use:

- AI project landscape;
- reviewed public signals;
- active source links;
- source-linked projects;
- public readiness signals;
- machine-readability evidence;
- reviewed sample counts;
- published projects;
- website reachable;
- GitHub linked;
- docs linked;
- sitemap detected;
- JSON-LD detected;
- canonical detected.

## Avoided Positioning

Do not frame `/landscape` as:

- a generic AI tools directory;
- a superlative tool list;
- the largest or complete market database;
- an official ranking;
- a paid placement surface;
- a generated SEO content farm;
- a commercial lead form;
- a live data feed.

## Forbidden Claim Types

Do not use:

- outcome promises for ranking, traffic, AI citation, ChatGPT visibility, collection, exposure, funding, or customers;
- investment-style or financial-advice framing;
- paid inclusion or automatic listing claims;
- backlink or search-value promises;
- official endorsement without source evidence;
- fake counts or unsupported global market counts;
- copied competitor descriptions.

## Allowed CTAs

- Explore reviewed projects.
- Browse stacks.
- Browse collections.
- Browse verticals.
- View approved alternatives.
- View reports.
- Read Alpha Feed evidence.
- Run geo-checker.
- Submit a project for human review.
- Claim/correct a public profile.

## Forbidden CTAs

- paid listing;
- paid ranking;
- customer signup;
- buyer-interest capture;
- live feed access;
- automated contact or outreach;
- ad placement purchase;
- any CTA promising ranking, traffic, citation, collection, exposure, capital access, customers, or placement outcome.

## Empty-State Copy

Approved:

> 88CN currently shows a reviewed sample of public AI project signals. Counts are not global market estimates.

Rules:

- do not invent categories;
- do not show global counts;
- do not create empty task pages;
- do not imply broad coverage;
- link to reports and geo-checker when landscape data is sparse;
- invite submit/correction through the reviewed workflow only.
