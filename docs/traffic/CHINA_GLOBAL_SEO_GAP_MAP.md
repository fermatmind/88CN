# TRAFFIC0R China / Global SEO Gap Map

## Thesis

China has active AI tool directories. The gap for 88CN is not another generic `AI工具导航`; it is a bilingual AI project landscape focused on reviewed public signals, lifecycle/source links, open-source/commercial split, machine-readability evidence, and outbound-founder research.

## Global Pattern Map

| Global pattern | Evidence from sampled competitors | SEO value | Risk | 88CN response |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| "AI tools directory" homepage | Toolify, Futurepedia, FutureTools, AIxploria | High broad discovery | Crowded, generic, easy to copy poorly | Avoid positioning as generic directory. |
| Category pages | Toolify categories, Futurepedia categories, AIxploria categories | Strong navigation and SEO | Can become thin or unbounded | Use finite route families with reviewed project thresholds. |
| Latest/new tools pages | Toolify new, FutureTools newly added, AIxploria latest | Freshness appeal | Stale listings and unverified freshness | Use reviewed update timestamps only. |
| Ranking pages | Toolify ranking/revenue, AIxploria top language | Acquisition potential | High trust and claim risk | Avoid unreviewed rankings; label sample counts only. |
| Alternatives pages | SaaSHub/AlternativeTo strategic pattern, Futurepedia detail pages | High acquisition | Duplicate/N-by-N risk | Canonical allowlist only. |
| Submit/listing pages | Toolify, Futurepedia, FutureTools, AIxploria, AIToolsDirectory | Founder acquisition | Paid inclusion or visibility promise risk | Human-reviewed submit/claim/correction with no outcome promise. |
| Editorial process pages | Futurepedia | Trust | Requires real process discipline | TRAFFIC1 should include review/lifecycle/source policy. |
| Education/newsletter pages | Futurepedia, FutureTools | Distribution | Can drift into content factory | Use reviewed reports and human-owned distribution packs. |

## First-Wave Route Recommendation

Build taxonomy first, then route surfaces:

1. TRAFFIC1 taxonomy.
2. `/landscape` as hub.
3. `/landscape/sectors` after sector definitions.
4. Limited first task pages for AI coding workflows, RAG knowledge base, local LLM deployment, and AI search readiness.
5. Limited alternatives allowlist.
6. `/zh-CN/landscape`, `/zh-CN/geo-checker`, `/zh-CN/founders`, `/zh-CN/submit` after stable English route strategy.

## Route Indexability Gate

Recommended default:

- Indexable only when a route has at least 3 reviewed/published projects.
- Otherwise `noindex, follow`.
- No sitemap entry while under threshold.
- Counts must be labeled as reviewed sample counts.
- Submitted but unreviewed pages must never enter sitemap.
