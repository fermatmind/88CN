Result: GO_TRAFFIC2

## Strategy

Chinese AI discovery is active and crowded. TRAFFIC1 does not position 88CN as another generic `AI工具导航`. The first Chinese traffic surface should translate the stable 88CN strategy: a bilingual AI project landscape focused on reviewed public signals, active source links, lifecycle status, source-backed open-source/commercial labels, and machine-readability evidence.

## Avoid First-Wave Direct Competition

Avoid building first-wave routes around:

- `AI工具`
- `AI工具导航`
- `AI工具大全`
- `AI工具集合`

Reason: these clusters are broad, crowded, and pull 88CN toward volume-first directory behavior. They may be mentioned only in careful explanatory context, not used as the primary first-wave route promise.

## Wording Risk Notes

`AI工具怎么被ChatGPT搜到`:

- Safe frame: a project page can be easier for crawlers and AI systems to understand when public source links, structured data, canonical tags, docs, and sitemaps are clear.
- Unsafe frame: any promise that ChatGPT or another AI system will cite, rank, include, recommend, or surface the project.

`GEO`:

- Safe frame: public readiness, machine readability, structured-data hygiene, source-link clarity.
- Unsafe frame: promotion-effect scoring, guaranteed AI answer inclusion, paid outcome claim.

`AI搜索可见性`:

- Safe frame: public signals that may help evaluators and crawlers understand a project.
- Unsafe frame: guaranteed visibility, traffic, ranking, collection, citation, or exposure.
